Tutorial

REI on the Fly is a program that allows you to analyze a potential investment property. Simply add a property your intersted in buying and see what you should offer. REIF will keep track of properties you are analyzing, offer National Average Costs to Remodel, and suggest the potential resell price of the property.

Begin by selecting New Property above and entering the details about the property. From there you can see a suggested purchase price and options to remodel the property. REIF will do the rest so you can decide if the deal is profitable before you buy. If you want to send a client a report choose "Send Report" from the CMA page.
